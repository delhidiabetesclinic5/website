Our Services

We offer complete, compassionate care for chronic and lifestyle-related conditions.

Diabetes Care

Advanced management of Type 1, Type 2, and gestational diabetes with CGM integration.

Hypertension

Blood pressure monitoring and lifestyle-centered treatment plans.

ABPM: Ambulatory BP Monitoring facility available.

Thyroid & PCOD Disorders

Comprehensive evaluation and management of thyroid and PCOD (Polycystic Ovarian Disease) disorders using advanced diagnostic tests, hormonal assessments, and personalized treatment plans for balanced health and improved well-being.

Neuropathy

Comprehensive assessment and management of neuropathy through advanced neurological evaluations, nerve conduction studies, and individualized treatment protocols aimed at reducing nerve pain, restoring function, and enhancing quality of life.

Fatty Liver Treatment

Integrated evaluation and treatment of fatty liver disease using advanced liver function testing, imaging diagnostics, and personalized lifestyle and dietary interventions to reduce liver fat, support liver health, and prevent progression to more serious liver conditions.
